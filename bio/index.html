What decision engineering actually is

Industrialized data science. Data science in a notebook produces findings; data science in production produces profitable decisions, at scale, repeatedly, with measurement infrastructure that keeps them profitable tomorrow. Every business is a decision factory — pricing, credit, fraud, compliance, allocation, reconciliation. The question isn't whether those decisions vary; they always vary. The question is whether the variance is controlled (design of experiments, A/B tests, the Karpathy loop) or uncontrolled (drift, stale data, inconsistent interpretations). Decision engineering builds the ontology and measurement infrastructure that turns agentic speed into compounded quality instead of compounded drift.
