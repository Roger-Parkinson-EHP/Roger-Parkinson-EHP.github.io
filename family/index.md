## The Headlines

- **Geoffrey Chaucer** (father of English literature) is a direct ancestor
- **3 Kings of England** — [Edward III](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Edward_III_of_England), [Henry V](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Henry_V_of_England), [Henry VII](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Henry_VII_of_England)
- **69 ancestors buried at [Westminster Abbey](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Westminster_Abbey)**
- **6 ancestors killed at the [Battle of Agincourt](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Agincourt)** (1415)
- **[Thomas Edwin Ricks](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thomas_E._Ricks_(Mormon_pioneer))** (direct ancestor) **founded Rexburg, Idaho** — where Roger was born
- DNA traces to **[Niall of the Nine Hostages](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Niall_of_the_Nine_Hostages)** (paternal) and **Benjamin Franklin** (maternal haplogroup)
- Paternal line lived in the epicenter of the **[Pendle witch trials](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pendle_witches)** (1612)
- Maternal line traces 8 generations on **[Gotland](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gotland)**, Sweden's Viking island
